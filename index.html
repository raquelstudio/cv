My name is Raquel Gomes, I was born in the Azores, but came to Lisbon to study Architecture, completing a master's degree and, afterwards, started to work as a freelance illustrator. I finally settled in Communication Design, in Lisbon University School of Fine Arts (FBAUL), a field that arose in me a passion like no other. It was in communication design that I found a place where I can fully explore my more subversive and rebelious streak. After finishing my bachelor's degree, I am now enrolled in an MA in Communication Design in FBAUL.
